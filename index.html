Paddock to Plate

Our animals are free to roam in open paddocks with full access to fresh water and shade from native trees.

We use no Bovaer, growth promotors or hormones for a natural, healthy end product.

Our cattle are grass fed, taking longer to mature as a result, but good results are worth the wait.

Slow grown beef is leaner and healthier than grain fed alternatives.

Reared sustainably in the Southern Highlands, our beef accumulates very few food miles as it is born and raised in Burradoo, processed in Picton and butchered in Bowral.
