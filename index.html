About Us

It is a well-known fact that most of Australia's best quality beef is exported to countries who are willing to pay exorbitant rates. Put simply, this means that the average Joe in Australia is getting second-rate beef, whether he likes it or not, and we think this is ridiculous. The Beef Club is a business dedicated to sourcing the best beef in Australia, for Australians. We constantly strive to find ways of ensuring the supply is direct so that prices can be kept reasonable. We love our country, we love our farmers, we love our customers, and we love what we do - finding and supplying the best beef in Australia, direct to your door.
